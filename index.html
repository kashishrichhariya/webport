About

Spaces that carry the weight of meaning

I design interiors for retail and hospitality — spaces that hold more than they show. Each project begins with a question about place, culture, and the person who will inhabit it. The answer shapes everything: material, proportion, light.

Working across three Tanishq flagships, I've learned that the most enduring spaces borrow from tradition without imitating it — where heritage is distilled, not displayed.
